Tiger Evolution

Here you can find the details of tiger evolution done through checking their genetic behaviour over the years with other subfamilies of tiger. You can also find the whether or not they need special conservation Unit.

  • Description
  • Statistics
  • Conclusion

Since the number of tigers have been on diminishing rise on the sunderbans there was an angle generated if these tigers have some habitable issues & that can happen if the current tigers are not of bengal tiger origins.​So, there was research done to study the evolution pattern of the Sunderbans tigers.​The research helped in finding if there was a migration ever happened from mainland land India to Sunderbans & also make a special conservation Unit for tigers.​

Researchers took tiger skulls in a mix from bangladesh & also included skulls of the extinct subspecies & found that sunderban subspecies skulls were comparatively smaller & lighter when compared to other subspecies. ​
